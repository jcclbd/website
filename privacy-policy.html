Privacy Policy

Jashore City Cable Pvt. Ltd. is committed to protecting the privacy and personal information of our customers. This privacy policy sets forth our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of customer information. By using the services provided by Jashore City Cable Pvt. Ltd., you agree to the terms of this privacy policy.
Information Collection and Use
Jashore City Cable Pvt. Ltd. collects personal information from customers when they subscribe to our services. This information may include, but is not limited to, names, addresses, telephone numbers, and payment information. Jashore City Cable Pvt. Ltd. uses this information to provide customers with cable TV services, process payments, and communicate with customers regarding their accounts.


Disclosure of Information
Jashore City Cable Pvt. Ltd. does not sell or rent customer information to third parties. We may, however, share customer information with trusted third parties who provide services on our behalf, such as billing and customer service. These third parties are obligated to maintain the confidentiality of customer information and use it only for the purpose of providing services to Jashore City Cable Pvt. Ltd.
We may also disclose customer information when required by law, such as in response to a court order or subpoena.


Data Security
Jashore City Cable Pvt. Ltd. takes the security of customer information seriously and has implemented appropriate measures to protect customer information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of data transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of customer information.

Changes to Privacy Policy
Jashore City Cable Pvt. Ltd. reserves the right to change this privacy policy at any time. If we make any changes, we will post the revised policy on our website and update the effective date.
